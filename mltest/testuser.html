Welcome to the Multiple Intelligence Test!


Multiple Intelligences is a theory developed by psychologist Howard Gardner. It suggests that intelligence is not a single entity that can be measured by a single IQ score, but rather a combination of different intelligences or abilities. According to Gardner, there are eight distinct intelligences:

  • linguistic
  • logical-mathematical
  • spatial
  • musical
  • bodily-kinesthetic
  • interpersonal
  • intrapersonal
  • naturalistic

Each person possesses a unique combination of these intelligences, and they can excel in different areas based on their strengths. This theory challenges the traditional view of intelligence and encourages educators to recognize and foster different types of intelligence in their teaching methods. In this quiz, you will have the opportunity to explore your own multiple intelligences and discover your areas of strength. Enjoy the quiz and have fun!
